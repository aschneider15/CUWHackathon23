Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cornerstone?

Cornerstone is a free decentralized social media application made for alumni to connect with their fellows and fund Concordia University Wisconsin Projects.


Is Cornerstone free to use?

Yes, Cornerstone only takes a BitPay wallet to authenticate users. While special features can be obtained by providing a CUW email, the site is open to anyone.


How do I donate to Projects?

Cornerstone uses BitPay to allow users to deposit funds to Projects. All it takes is your private key and a specified donation amount.


How do I comment on Cornerstone?

Posting content is easy! All it takes is to navigate to the Homepage and find a project that interests you. From there, the box at the bottom should allow you to input text.


Can I use Cornerstone for business and commercial purposes?

Yes, we'd love to have new Projects mentioned and promoted on our site.


How do I stay informed on Cornerstone's latest news and updates?

Information can be found on the CUW Blog, linked here:  https://blog.cuw.edu/
